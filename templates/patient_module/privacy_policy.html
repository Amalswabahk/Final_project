1. Introduction

HealthConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our patient portal.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information
  • Name, date of birth, and contact details
  • Insurance information
  • Emergency contact information
Health Information
  • Medical history and conditions
  • Medications and allergies
  • Lab results and test reports
  • Treatment plans and physician notes
Technical Information
  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage data and access times

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage your healthcare services
  • Communicate with you about appointments and care
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Improve our services and portal functionality
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain the security of our systems

4. Information Disclosure

We may disclose your information in these circumstances:

  • To healthcare providers involved in your treatment
  • When required by law or legal process
  • In emergency situations to protect health or safety
  • To insurance companies for payment purposes
  • To business associates under HIPAA-compliant agreements

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity
While we implement these security measures, no system can be 100% secure. You should also take steps to protect your account credentials.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Request access to your health records
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request restrictions on certain uses/disclosures
  • Request an accounting of disclosures
  • Obtain a copy of this privacy notice

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze portal usage and performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect portal functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our portal is not designed for children under 13. For minors aged 13-17, we comply with all applicable laws regarding health information privacy for minors.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the portal or via email.
